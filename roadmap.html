Roadmap

We divide our next year road map in four phases :

Phase I - Simple betting Futebol game

Objective:

This first phase will serve as a proof of concepts (POC) to test the feasibility of the requirements of our future community. For the web version the goal is to engage the EOS community with a real application and create design and architectural standards. With the mobile version the goal is to enable the creation of new EOS accounts and reach users with little experience in crypto. The focus is on potential users who have their currencies deposited in exchanges or who can be targeted directly by those experienced in the crypto world.
