Mission

GoalBarClub mission is to be the best environment where football/soccer fans can join an independent community and bet in games based on the sport they love.

We aim to foster community independence and prevent that forces outside of our community interfere. We will adopt the EOS governance principles to start the first football pool community, self-governed, with best rewards distribution when compared with any other betting site approach.

In most lotteries, casino or gambling sites, only a small part of the total amount raised gets distributed to the winners. At GoalBarClub, we want to grow the community and add value based on the network effect as more people join our community.

The goalbarclub project is meant to be a decentralized betting club. In this sense, the majority part of what will be collected on our games, will be distributed directly to the winners of each contest.

The project is not for profit, so there is not a part retained for this purpose. To further gather bettors and create an aggregate community we will create the GOAL token that will be distributed to the community in different ways. From the total collected on each contest, about 20% will be retained with two objectives:

  • Cover application marketing, game development, legal costs and operation costs ( RAM/CPU space)
  • Distribute as a daily reward, in proportion to the amount of tokens of each user, all the rest of what is retained.

As our user base expands, members that joined early on will benefit from the token value appreciation.
