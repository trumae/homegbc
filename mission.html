Mission

GoalBarClub mission is to be the best environment where football/soccer fans can join an independent community and bet in games based on the sport they love. We will act like a betting club and also allow members to create their lottery games based on the important world football championships games.

We aim to foster community independence and prevent that forces outside of our community interfere. We are adopting the EOS governance principles to start the first football lottery community, self-governed, with best rewards distribution when compared with any other lottery.

In most lotteries, only a small part of the total amount raised gets distributed to the winners. At GoalBarClub, we want to grow the community and add value based on the network effect as more people join our community.

We will charge a relatively small fee to cover the operational costs and deposit the proceeds on the community accounts. We plan to engage the community and involve power users in helping us to make our group stronger.

As our user base expands, members that joined early on will benefit from the token value appreciation.
